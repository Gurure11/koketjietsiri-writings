About Us



**Koketjie Tsiri** is an accomplished author, pastor, preacher, anointed teacher of God's word, prophetess, and administrator dedicated to transforming lives.
With years of devoted service, she has been a beacon of hope and inspiration for many through her impactful writings and engaging seminars.

Koketjie's journey is marked by an unwavering commitment to spreading the teachings of God and empowering individuals to live fulfilling lives. As a pastor and preacher,
her sermons resonate deeply with her congregation, offering profound insights and spiritual guidance. Her anointed teaching brings clarity and understanding to the scriptures,
fostering a deeper connection with faith.

A gifted prophetess, Koketjie delivers messages of hope, encouragement, and divine direction, helping many navigate life's challenges with renewed faith. Her administrative skills ensure
the smooth operation of her ministries, allowing her to reach a broader audience effectively.



Through her books and seminars, Koketjie Tsiri continues to inspire and uplift, making a lasting impact on countless lives. Her dedication to changing lives is a testament to her faith and
passion for serving others.
